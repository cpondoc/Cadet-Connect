About

This project documents all of the clubs and honor societies here at Frederick High School.


Motivation


As a freshman, high school can assuredly feel overwhelming. From much more rigorous classes to a new schedule, new students have to adjust to a lot. Perhaps one of the more complex and impactful decisions is joining a club or honor society for the first time. For many students, this can be a chance to explore new passions, meet new people, and contribute to the many initiatives Frederick High students are pursuing.

While we do hold a club fair at the beginning of the year, some students may not always enjoy a club after the first couple of weeks, while some might want to explore new options. This site allows students to not limit themselves to two days during their lunch period.
